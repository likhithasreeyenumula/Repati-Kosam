# 💊 Repati Kosam

## Inspiration

Millions of people, especially elderly individuals and patients with chronic illnesses, struggle to remember their medications, maintain medical records, and respond quickly during emergencies. We were inspired by real-life situations where missed medicines led to health complications and where family members worried about their loved ones' wellbeing. This motivated us to create **Repati Kosam**, which means **"For Tomorrow"** in Telugu, a platform focused on helping people stay healthy today for a better tomorrow.

## What it does

Repati Kosam is an AI-powered healthcare assistant that helps users:

* Manage medication schedules and dosage information.
* Receive medicine reminders.
* Store and organize medical records digitally.
* Maintain emergency contacts for quick access.
* Track medication history and adherence.
* Access an AI assistant for healthcare guidance.
* Trigger SOS alerts during emergencies.

The platform provides a simple and user-friendly interface that supports patients, caregivers, and elderly users in managing their health effectively.

## How we built it

We built Repati Kosam as a web-based healthcare platform using:

* **HTML5** for page structure.
* **CSS3** for responsive and attractive user interface design.
* **JavaScript** for interactive functionality.
* AI Assistant module for health-related guidance and support.

The project consists of multiple integrated modules including:

* User Authentication
* Dashboard
* Medication Management
* Medication History
* Medical Records
* Emergency Contact System
* AI Assistant
* Settings and Preferences

We focused on creating a clean healthcare-themed design with easy navigation and accessibility.

## Challenges we ran into

During development, we faced several challenges:

* Designing a user-friendly interface suitable for both young and elderly users.
* Organizing multiple healthcare modules into a single seamless platform.
* Creating an effective medication management workflow.
* Designing the emergency support system and SOS functionality.
* Ensuring consistency in navigation, styling, and user experience across all pages.

Balancing functionality with simplicity was one of the biggest challenges throughout the project.
